APA 參考資料

理論與實務來源

本專題使用的主要參考

以下以 APA 第七版格式整理，方便課堂報告或書面說明直接引用。

一、年輕人職涯探索與生涯發展

介紹 18–25 歲、剛開始規劃職涯的年輕人在選擇科系與工作方向時，常見的發展任務與迷惘來源。

  • Super, D. E. (1990). A life-span, life-space approach to career development. In D. Brown, L. Brooks, & Associates (Eds.), Career choice and development (2nd ed., pp. 197–261). Jossey-Bass.
  • Savickas, M. L. (2013). Career construction theory and practice. In S. D. Brown & R. W. Lent (Eds.), Career development and counseling: Putting theory and research to work (2nd ed., pp. 147–183). Wiley.
  • Nota, L., & Rossier, J. (Eds.). (2015). Handbook of life design: From practice to theory and from theory to practice. Hogrefe.

二、性格測驗與人格測量（MBTI 等）

說明性格測驗與人格工具在職涯輔導中的使用與限制。

  • Myers, I. B., McCaulley, M. H., Quenk, N. L., & Hammer, A. L. (1998). MBTI manual: A guide to the development and use of the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (3rd ed.). Consulting Psychologists Press.
  • Nauta, M. M. (2010). The development, evolution, and status of Holland’s theory of vocational personalities: Reflections and future directions for counseling psychology. The Counseling Psychologist, 38(1), 11–35. https://doi.org/10.1177/0011000009357087

三、職涯探索介入與活動設計

為本專題中的遊戲、迷宮與互動練習提供理論與實務靈感。

  • Hirschi, A., & Läge, D. (2007). The relation of secondary students’ career-choice readiness to a six-phase model of career decision-making. Journal of Career Development, 34(2), 164–191. https://doi.org/10.1177/0894845307307473
  • Turner, S. L., & Lapan, R. T. (2005). Evaluation of an intervention to increase non-traditional career interests and career-related self-efficacy among middle-school adolescents. Journal of Vocational Behavior, 66(3), 516–531. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jvb.2004.02.005
  • Nota, L., Santilli, S., & Soresi, S. (2016). A life-design-based online career intervention for early adolescents: Description and initial analysis. Career Development Quarterly, 64(1), 4–19. https://doi.org/10.1002/cdq.12037

四、華語地區實務資源（網站）

若要延伸課堂活動，可從下列網站取得最新職涯與科系資訊。

  • 勞動部勞動力發展署（n.d.）。台灣就業通：職涯探索與職業資訊。 取自 https://www.taiwanjobs.gov.tw
  • 教育部技術及職業教育司（n.d.）。技職地圖與校系資訊平台。 取自 https://www.techphoto.moe.gov.tw
  • Youth.gov.hk（n.d.）。Career & life planning。 取自 https://www.youth.gov.hk

如果你在報告或教案中引用了本網站內容，也歡迎一併標註這些原始參考資料。
